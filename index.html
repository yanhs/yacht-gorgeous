Safety & Policies

🛟 Safety On Board

Your safety is our top priority. All cruises aboard Gorgeous are operated by a professional captain and follow safety procedures to ensure a comfortable and secure journey.

  • 🛟 Life jackets and safety equipment available on board
  • ⚓ Licensed captain with navigation experience
  • 👥 Maximum recommended capacity: 12 guests
  • 👶 Children must be supervised by adults
  • 🚫 Please avoid standing on railings or unsafe areas
  • 📢 In case of emergency follow the captain's instructions

📋 Guest Rules & Policies

Guest Guidelines

  • ⏰ Please arrive 10–15 minutes before departure
  • 👥 Maximum recommended capacity: 12 guests
  • ⚓ Follow the captain's instructions at all times
  • 🤝 Please respect the yacht and other guests
  • 🚬 Smoking only in designated areas
  • ⚠️ The captain reserves the right to refuse service if behavior is unsafe

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made 48 hours before departure are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations made less than 48 hours before the cruise may not be refunded.

Weather Policy

In case of unsafe weather conditions, the cruise may be rescheduled or refunded.

General inquiries

Have a question?

For private events, custom routes, or anything outside the booking flow — leave a note and we'll write back.
